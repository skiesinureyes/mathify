What is Mental Math?

Mental math refers to the ability to perform arithmetic calculations in your head without the aid of calculators, paper, or other tools. It involves using strategies and techniques to solve problems quickly and accurately, relying on your knowledge of numbers and mathematical concepts.

Mental math improves calculation speed, sharpens memory and concentration, enhances number sense, boosts problem-solving skills, and builds confidence in handling math tasks quickly and independently.
